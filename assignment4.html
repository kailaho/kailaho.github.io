Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

In challenges 2-4, there are several features that are potential accessibility concerns. Most noteably, challenge 2 is created using a table, which are notoriously difficult for screen readers to process. Anyone who is blind would have a very difficult time understanding what is happening in this section of the website. Another issue could arise again in challenge 4 because a table is used again, which might cause more problems with screen readers. The form in challenge 3 could also be difficult for someone with mobility difficulties. The check boxes and text boxes might be difficult for some people to interact with for this reason.

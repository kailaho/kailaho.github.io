Final Project: Word Collage

This interactive tool allows you enter a paragraph or series of words into a text box. It then analyzes the paragraph you eneter, and increases the size of the printed word by the number of times it occurs. It also generates a random color for the text, allowing you to create a colorful collage displaying the frequency of certain words in a visual way. You can click the "Get Collage" button as many times as you want, until you are happy with how it looks.
